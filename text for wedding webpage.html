Tips for travelling

For those of you who need to come by plane, we can recommend momondo.com to find the cheapest ones. Copenhagen airport is only a 15min train or metro ride away from the city centre, if you get off at the main station or at Norreport you should be able to change to a metro or bus that take you to your accomodation, maybe you can even walk from there. As an alternative to Hotel- search webpages you might want to have a look at air bnb, a webpage where rooms in or entire private apartments can be rented. Anyplace in the centre should be ok to reach the venues, vesterbro, frederiksberg or the centre are very nice areas to stay.
