Accommodation

There are many options for staying in Copenhagen and they come in all prices. If you stay in City (København K) or Vesterbro (København V) you will be very central and close to both the church and the dinner venue

To our friends in Copenhagen

We think it would be really nice if our friends got to know each other well. Therefore, we would like to encourage our friends in Copenhagen with an extra bedroom (not too common in Copenhagen - we know) to invite friends travelling from abroad to come and stay.

Please, contact us if you can and would like to host guests travelling far.

Other ways to stay as the locals

Try airbnb.com if you want to stay as the Copenhageners. People on Airbnb will let you rent their apartment for a few nights and we can absolutely recommend it.

Hotel Cabinn

Cabinn is a very affordable option but the cheapest rooms can be very small.

First Hotel Twentyseven

First Hotel Twentyseven is a step up, trendy, central and still very affordable.

Getting around Copenhagen

From the airport

Copenhagen Airport is located very close to the city. It only takes 14 minutes to get to the city.

If want to go to the central station or Vesterbro, take any train from the airport train station.

If you want to go to Nørreport or the central city, take the metro.

Taxi from the airport to central Copenhagen is 200-250 kr., 26-30 €.

Rent a bicycle

Copenhageners love their bicycles and it really is the best way to get around in the city.

Rent a refurbished bike at Baisikeli and support their social work in Mozambique.
